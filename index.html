Our Mission

To strengthen Africa’s ability to generate and use trustworthy data and AI for public good — from ethical data collection and stewardship to rigorous analysis and secure deployment.

Focus Areas

Datasets

Design, documentation, and governance of open, high-quality datasets fit for research and policy.

AI for Public Interest

Transparent models for health, education, agriculture, finance, and civic services.

Cybersecurity

Secure infrastructure, awareness training, and risk-informed practices across institutions.

Capacity Building

Mentorship, seminars, and hands-on labs that develop researchers and practitioners.
